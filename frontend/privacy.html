Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Dants Arena collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website, create an account, book a pitch, or join a membership.

Information We Collect

  • Your name, email address, phone number, username, and account details.
  • Booking, membership, and payment-related information needed to run the service.
  • Basic technical information needed to keep the site secure and working properly.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To process bookings, memberships, and support requests.
  • To send confirmations, updates, and important account notices.
  • To improve the platform and maintain security.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with service providers that help us run the platform, such as payments, hosting, and email delivery.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact Dants Arena directly before using the service further.
