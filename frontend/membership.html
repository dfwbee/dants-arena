Common Questions

FAQs

Can I cancel my membership anytime? +
Yes. You can cancel your membership at any time with no penalty. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? +
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan from your account dashboard at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing date.
When will the gym and lounge open? +
The gym and lounge are currently under construction. Members who sign up now will get early access when they open. We'll notify you via email.
How does the QR code work? +
Your QR code is your digital membership card. Show it at the reception desk when you arrive. It also verifies your booking for each session.
Do unused bookings roll over? +
No, unused bookings do not roll over to the next month. We recommend booking in advance to make the most of your plan.
